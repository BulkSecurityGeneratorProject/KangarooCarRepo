Step 1

Register

Sign up to Carshare. It takes less than a minute. No personal details are required and no credit card is needed as we don't take a penny from you. All you need is an email address.

Step 2

Find

If you are a driver you can advertise your journey and passengers will bid for a seat. Alternatively, if you don't drive or you just don't feel like driving you can advertise a journey request and let the drivers contact you.

Step 3

Share

Meet up at the agreed location and time, pay the driver the amount you bid and share the journey. After you reach your destination feel free to review any other person on the journey.

What We Do

We provide a car sharing service, where users can register and post their journeys and request a seat. Fellow users can log on to the site and bid for the posted journeys and journey requests. If a bid is successful, the user is contacted by email. Once a journey is complete, the participants are allowed to provide a review. With this free service there is no need to provide personal information.
